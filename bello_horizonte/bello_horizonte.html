Bello Horizonte Beach



An area very frequented by tourists is the beaches of Bello Horizonte, in the west of Santa Marta. It is an area that is characterized by the construction of multi-story hotels and tourist residences in which visitors find a wide variety of restaurants and entertainment venues. This is a good place to travel with the family.
