What To Do in Bello Horizonte Beach?



  • Paddle Surf


    Paddle Surf is a form of gliding in which the surfer uses a paddle to navigate the water while standing on a surfboard. It is a wonderful experience to travel the beach on a surfboard quietly.


    Providers:

    On this beach, you can get the necessary equipment to practice paddle in the hotels that are located around it. You can also hire the service with companies such as Caribean Sailing School and Paddle Santa Marta.

  • Windsurf


    Windsurfing consists of sliding over the water on a board that carries a sail. Windsurf boards are light, fast and easy to handle.


    Providers:

    In Bello Horizonte, you can learn windsurfing at the Caribbean Sailing School, or acquire the necessary equipment at the Zuana hotel.

  • Kayaking


    Kayaking is a watersport that involves paddling using a double-bladed oar and a small boat known as a kayak.


    Providers:

    The hotels around the beach provide kayak rental services. You can also find local tour operators that offer the service.

  • Jet Skis


    Jet skis are a type of light boat with a driving system similar to that of a conventional motorcycle, with the aim of having fun for a while while touring the beach.


    Providers:

    You can purchase the jetskis rental service with the different tour operators that are on the beach.
