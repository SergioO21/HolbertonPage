Playa Grande Beach



Playa Grande is beautiful, with transparent waters and beautiful corals, with numerous kiosks, cabins and hotels in which it is possible to enjoy a delicious sea food and admire the environment, its access is by boat from Taganga or by ecological trail. Taganga is a small fishing village located about 15 minutes, there you can also find apartments to stay.
