What To Do in Playa Grande Beach?



  • Snorkeling


    This activity is characterized by the use of a diving mask and a breathing tube called a snorkel. It is also necessary to wear diving fins on the feet to propel the body while the person is submerged in the sea.


    Providers:

    It is an activity in which it is not necessary to be an expert, but around the beach there are several tour operators who will help you and provide you with the necessary equipment.

  • Scuba Diving


    Scuba diving is a mode of underwater diving where the diver uses a self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (scuba), which is completely independent of surface supply, to breathe underwater.


    Providers:

    In Playa Grande you can dive through these schools: Poseidon Dive Center, Barracuda Dive Center, Nautilus Dive Center and more

  • Boat Travel


    Boat trips are ideal for when you want to move from one beach to another in the company of your family or friends, while enjoying the seascape.


    Providers:

    In Playa Grande, the boatmen who come from Taganga beach also offer the boat ride service for this sector.

  • Kayaking


    Kayaking is a watersport that involves paddling using a double-bladed oar and a small boat known as a kayak.


    Providers:

    The local tour operators will be kindly waiting for you to enjoy a kayak trip.
