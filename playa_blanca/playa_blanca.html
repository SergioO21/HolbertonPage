Playa Blanca Beach



It must be said that the name of Playa Blanca is very appropriate. This point in the north of the bay of Santa Marta, Colombia, is reached from the beaches of El Rodadero after a twenty-minute boat ride. Once there, visitors can practice water sports and find various food options, as well as accommodation services.
