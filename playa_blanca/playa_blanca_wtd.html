What To Do in Playa Blanca Beach?



  • Snorkeling


    This activity is characterized by the use of a diving mask and a breathing tube called a snorkel. It is also necessary to wear diving fins on the feet to propel the body while the person is submerged in the sea.


    Providers:

    Through the Adrenaline Colombia agency, you can practice snorkeling around the beach safely and with the appropriate equipment.

  • Boat Travel


    Boat trips are ideal for when you want to move from one beach to another in the company of your family or friends, while enjoying the seascape.


    Providers:

    Taxi Marino is one of the best tour operators that you can find in the rodadero beach, and it also provides boat trip services for this beach, so you will not have any problem when purchasing it.

  • Jet Skis


    Jet skis are a type of light boat with a driving system similar to that of a conventional motorcycle, with the aim of having fun for a while while touring the beach.


    Providers:

    The tourist providers for the activities in Playa Blanca are located on the Rodadero Beach.

  • Nautical Charter


    The nautical charter is the activity of renting pleasure boats with or without a skipper.


    Providers:

    The tourist providers for the activities in Playa Blanca are located on the Rodadero Beach.
