What To Do in Taganga Beach?



  • Kayaking


    Kayaking is a watersport that involves paddling using a double-bladed oar and a small boat known as a kayak.


    Providers:

    In taganga you can find several local tour operators around the beach, with the help of them you can practice this activity.

  • Scuba Diving


    Scuba diving is a mode of underwater diving where the diver uses a self-contained underwater breathing apparatus (scuba), which is completely independent of surface supply, to breathe underwater.


    Providers:

    There are different diving schools in Taganga where you can attend, such as: Oceano Scuba Dive Center, Calipso Dive Center, Tayrona Dive Center, and more.

  • Nautical Charter


    The nautical charter is the activity of renting pleasure boats with or without a skipper.


    Providers:

    There are different internet pages where you can rent pleasure boats, such as: Nauty360, Nautal, Boats4U, Alquiler de Yates Santa Marta, and more.

  • Boat Travel


    Boat trips are ideal for when you want to move from one beach to another in the company of your family or friends, while enjoying the seascape.


    Providers:

    In the surroundings of Taganga beach, you can find local boatmen who will give you a ride for a very affordable price.
