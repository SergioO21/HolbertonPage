Taganga Beach



Taganga is a district of Santa Marta , in Magdalena ( Colombia ). 2 It has 3,000 inhabitants. It is made up of a town on the shores of the bay of the same name whose traditional activity has been fishing, although recently tourism has gained great prominence. Access is possible by road, from the center of Santa Marta.
