What To Do in El Rodadero Beach?



  • Jet Skis


    Jet skis are a type of light boat with a driving system similar to that of a conventional motorcycle, with the aim of having fun for a while while touring the beach.


    Providers:

    In the rodadero beach, in addition to finding local tour operators, you can rent a jet ski with companies such as Surfextreme Jetski or Club Nautico Mar Azul.

  • Boat Travel


    Boat trips are ideal for when you want to move from one beach to another in the company of your family or friends, while enjoying the seascape.


    Providers:

    On the rodadero beach, the Taxi Marino company is the ideal one to have the opportunity to visit the rest of the city's beaches.

  • Nautical Charter


    The nautical charter is the activity of renting pleasure boats with or without a skipper.


    Providers:

    The Go Colombia tourism agency offers luxury boat rental services to enjoy the maritime landscape with class.

  • Kayaking


    Kayaking is a watersport that involves paddling using a double-bladed oar and a small boat known as a kayak.


    Providers:

    You will have the opportunity to practice this activity with the company of different certified local tour operators that are on the beach
